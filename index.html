About Me

I'm a data analyst who traded lab coats for dashboards, and I couldn't be happier about it. With a BSc in Chemistry and Biochemistry from the University of Johannesburg (2023), a data analysis fellowship with ALX Africa, and currently pursuing a diploma in IT with UNISA, I've learned that whether you're analyzing chemical reactions or customer behavior, the thrill is the same: discovering patterns that others miss. My research background means I can't help but dig deeper — I don't just accept what the data shows on the surface; I ask "why?" and "what if?" until I find insights that actually matter. My sweet spot? Turning messy datasets into clear, compelling reports that help people make smarter decisions.

I'm currently helping a local business crack the code on their sales patterns and customer behavior, and there's something incredibly satisfying about watching them light up when they finally understand what's driving their numbers. I'm open to opportunities across different industries and roles, whether it's data analyst, business analyst, or anywhere my analytical skills can make an impact. When I'm not knee-deep in spreadsheets or building visualizations, I'm probably geeking out over a new tool or technique. The best part about data? There's always something new to learn, and I'm here for all of it.
